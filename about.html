Welcome to Our Conselling Center

Indra-pushp counselling center is a multidisciplinary, holistic, wellbeing focused mental health organization providing it's services in three main areas- Psychological Counselling - we offer psychotherapeutic counselling and psychometric test to the client dealing with stress, anxiety, depression, uncertainty, relationship and other mental health related issues.

Remedial Education - we are providing co-ordinated multidisciplinary care to children with Developmental Disabilities, school readiness program for learning disabled child.

The center takes necessary efforts to support students to complete their matriculation certificate by preparing them for National Institute of Open Schooling Examination.

Psycho-shiksha (Learn, Nurture, Progress)
We also offer psychology Tutorial for:
1. BA/MA/BSC (any University including Ignou)
2. Class xii and xi Psychology(all boards )
3. Internship and project guidence for psychology aspirants.

We have a excellent professional and experienced team with clinical psychologist, Rehabilitation psychologist,special educator, counselling psychologist , psychiatric counsellor .
Our aim is to assist Client to resolve crisis and life problems through personal change and fostering well being through personal growth.
